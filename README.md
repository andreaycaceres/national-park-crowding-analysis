# CSCI 5612-830 - National Park Crowding Analysis
This project explores U.S. National Park visitation data from 2015 to 2025, sourced from the National Park Service API. The goal is to better understand what drives visitation to national parks by analyzing factors such as camping activity, number of activities offered, entrance fees, and regional differences. Through exploratory data analysis, modeling, and visualization, this project uncovers trends over time, compares parks and regions, and examines how major events like COVID-19 and the Outdoor Boom shaped visitation patterns across the country.
