Introduction

National parks have experienced significant increases in visitation over the last several decades. Growing outdoor recreation, social media exposure, population growth, and changing travel patterns have contributed to crowding in many parks.

  6. What park characteristics predict visitation?
  7. Which parks are most vulnerable to overcrowding?
  8. Can future visitation be predicted?
  9. How do visitation trends differ by region?
  10. What role does hiking play in visitation growth?
